About Me

Hey there! I'm BM — a code-slinging, pixel-pushing creative with a deep love for all things tech, design, and... well, anything with a good plot twist or boss battle. When I'm not writing clean, scalable code or perfecting UI layouts, you’ll probably find me immersed in a sci-fi epic, grinding through a mission in Call of Duty, vibing to some music, or watching anime for “creative inspiration” (yes, that’s what I tell myself).

By day (and often night), I’m a Software Engineer and Graphics Designer who thrives at the intersection of logic and aesthetics. My journey into the digital world started with curiosity — how do games work? How do websites come to life? That curiosity quickly evolved into a mission: to build tech that not only works seamlessly but looks and feels amazing.

Whether I’m developing robust backend systems or designing smooth, engaging user experiences, I approach each project with the same mindset: tell a story, solve a problem, and leave a lasting impression. I see every line of code and every design element as part of a bigger narrative — one that’s meant to be both functional and unforgettable.
